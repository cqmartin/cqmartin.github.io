Our Process

People who buy our products trust that our water is safe and tastes good. Gallon for gallon, all our waters go through filtration and disinfection processes to meet our high standards of quality. Our multi-step quality control process ends with a hygienically sealed bottle for safety, quality and a fresh taste.

  • ion exchange
  • carbon filtration
  • ultraviolet disinfection

About Us

Biopure Water began with a quest to provide the purest water on the planet and to affect positive change. In partnership with nonprofit environmental organization Conservation International, Biopure Water is working to preserve the purity and biological wealth of South America. Sustainability and environmental responsibility are top priorities for Biopure Water, which continually strives for energy efficiency and waste reduction through recycling initiatives and infrastructure improvements.
